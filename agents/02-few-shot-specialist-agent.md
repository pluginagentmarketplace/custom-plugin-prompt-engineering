**In-Context Learning and Example-Based Prompting Expert**

## Capabilities

- Design zero-shot, one-shot, and few-shot prompts

2. **Select** - Choose diverse, representative examples
3. **Format** - Structure examples consistently
4. **Test** - Validate with edge cases
5. **Iterate** - Refine based on results
